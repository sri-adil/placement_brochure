    • MTech in Computer Science and Engineering

    The M.Tech degree in Computer Science (CSE) is intended to provide education for students who wish to work in the IT industry as practitioners. It is awarded upon successful completion of a 4-semester broad-based academic program in CSE, going beyond, but incorporating essential elements of, traditional computer science.

    Apart from imparting technical knowledge, the program teaches managerial and other cognate skills that are essential for a successful career in today's competitive IT industry. All students enrolled in the M.Tech program are residential, full-time students. The M.Tech. program is a four semester program. The first three semesters of the program constitute academic course work. During the fourth semester, a student can either take up an industry internship or academic research at the institute leading to a thesis.

    SAADI-EC: A Quality-Configurable Approximate Divider for Energy Efficiency

    This project aims at designing a divider, named Scalable Accuracy Approximate Divider with Error Compensation (SAADI-EC), supports dynamic energy-quality scalability by the incremental approximation of the reciprocal of the divisor. This project evaluates the accuracy and energy consumption of SAADI-EC and demonstrate its effectiveness for a low power signal processing application named K-means colour quantization.

    An energy efficient truncation based approximate multiplier(TOSAM)

    In the project, multiplication is performed by shift, add, and small _x000C_xed-width multiplication operations resulting in large improvements in the energy consumption and area occupation compared to those of the exact multiplier.

    Reconstruction of complex Compressed Sensed MRI images using cyclicGANs.

    The raw MRI images from the detector is subsampled and is having aliasing which can be reconstructed using deep learning methods like cyclicGAN.

    Elderly Human Monitoring based on Human Activity Recognition System using Accelerometer

    A Human Activity Recognition system was designed to monitor elderly people. Mobile mounted linear acceleration sensor was used along with machine learning models to predict activities

    Verification of design implementing various mathematical operations

    This project aims at writing test-bench for CDV of a design implementing square, square root, cube, cube root and factorial is written in System Verilog. It uses DPI to check results in score board. Tool used for this testbench is Xilinx Vivado 2019.2

    Dress recognition using pre trained YOLO and pipelining it with the image classifier.

    The person is detected using pre trained YOLO which is then passed through the image classifier which is trained on the dataset we created containing Indian ethnic wear.

    Design and Implementation of a Half-Select Free Write Assist 9T SRAM.

    Design and implementation of Low-Power single-ended 9T SRAM cell with read-decoupling and feedback cutting mechanism to achieve higher static noise margin.

    Implementation of a Wide-Voltage-Range Half-Path Timing Error- Detection System With a 9-Transistor Transition-Detector in 40-nm CMOS.

    This project aims to reduce conservative timing margin in digital circuit designs, adaptive techniques based on timing-error detection were proposed to monitor the timing of selected critical paths.

    ASIC Physical Design Flow of 8 Point DIT FFT Algorithm

    ASIC flow implementation of​ Fast Fourier transform​ (​FFT​) which is an algorithm that computes the Discrete Fourier Transform(DFT) of a sequence, or its inverse (IDFT). Fourier analysis converts a signal from time domain to frequency domain and vice versa.

    Verilog Implementation of SIMD PROCESSOR, Implementing DAXPY loop.

    Designing of 5 stage 16-bit SIMD processor using verilog HDL Instruction operates on multiple data elements in consecutive time steps making it faster than any other processor.

    Implementation of Hybrid Branch predictor in verilog HDL

    Implementation of a branch predictor with a large history table(Gshare predictor) which is combined with another predictor with a small or no history table(Bimodal Predictor).A Selector is used to select the prediction outcome from one of them and it is updated based on the prediction correctness of two predictors. Thus with the help of a hybrid branch predictor it is possible to exploit the different properties of the single-scheme components for a better overall performance.

    Packaging of Frequency Divider (ASIC)

    Synthesis, Pin placement, DRC errors correction and packaging of a Frequency Divider chip along with the padframe using open source tools on efabless.

    Implementation and Evaluation of Re-use Detection(ReD) Block Selection Policy for Last Level Cache (LLC).

    This Project aims at implementing ReD Block selection policy using Re-use locality principle to make LLC scan-resistant. It was tested using 5 different Trace files under 4 different configurations

    Verilog implementation of branch predictors and comparing their prediction accuracy

    This project aims at designing and implementing Different types of branch predictors using Verilog such as Tournament predictor, Correlated predictor, Two bit predictor, One bit predictor which are implemented on 2 nested loop written in MIPS and C. Finally their accuracies have been compared with inner loop performing 4 iteration and outer loop performing 1000 iterations.

    Functional Verification of 128-bit AES Pipelined Architecture Encryption Block

    Dual Threshold Voltage (Vt) Multiplier with Modified Dynamic Power Cutoff Technique

    A 4x4 array multiplier designed for low power and fast output. In order to get faster outputs, the critical path is designed with low Vt cells. The non-critical path is designed with high Vt cells in order to decrease the dynamic and leakage power. The DPCT cells are added in order to decrease the leakage power.

    People detection in crowd

    Low Phase Noise CMOS Quadrature VCO

    Quadrature CMOS VCO was designed from scratch operating at a centre frequency of 1.1GHz. The close in phase noise achieved at 10KHz offset was -99dBc/Hz. The phase noise at 1MHz offset is -141 dBc/Hz. Tuning range is approximately 7 percent with control voltage varying from 0 to 1.8V. The excellent FOM achieved is -195.8 dBc/Hz with the power dissipation of 4mW. The implementation of Quadrature VCO and its simulations were carried out in Cadence virtuoso using gpdk180 library.

    Implementation of the Cache Compression Algorithm, C-Pack

    In this verilog implementation,16 words’ input of cache lines have been losslessly compressed. The algorithm has been designed specifically for hardware on-chip compression. C-Pack uses statistically decided, compact encodings for frequently appearing data words, and it encodes using a dynamically updated dictionary allowing adaptation to other frequently appearing words. Two words per cycle are used as the input. It achieves a good compression ratio when used to compress data commonly found in microprocessor low-level on-chip caches like L2 caches.

    Efficient and Resilient Wireless Networks using UAVs

    Being UAVs, the base stations can be deployed in 3D space to maximize channel benefit and can rearrange themselves reacting to the network requirement. Furthermore, because of the ease of deployment, connectivity can be restored quickly when it's interrupted, for example, in natural disaster.
    The objective was to design a channel model to help identify the best 3D arrangement to maximize channel benefit.

    Master of Science by Research/PhD

    The Institute offers two research programmes for students that lay emphasis on carrying out quality research in the chosen field of study.

    The Master of Science degree is intended for mature students who wish to learn and perform research in a supportive academic environment. It is awarded upon successful completion of a graduate-level research programme, usually lasting four semesters. Beyond the satisfaction of a relatively small number of coursework requirements, the major focus of the program is on developing research skills, leading to the completion of a Master's thesis describing significant original results. Most M.S. by Research students are working professionals (some already working as research scientists in reputed organizations or government bodies), who are sponsored by their employers.

    The Doctor of Philosophy is the terminal, research-oriented degree in the subject, intended to prepare students for research, teaching, and scholarly careers in academic settings or research laboratories. It connotes a superior comprehension of the field and a high aptitude for research, and is awarded upon completion of a program that takes from 3 to 5 years (there are no hard limits). Beyond the satisfaction of a relatively small number of coursework requirements, the major focus is on carrying out a significant body of original research and the writing and defense of a doctoral dissertation describing this work.
